About Me

I specialize in designing energy-efficient and performant visual computing systems, with a focus on AR/VR and IoT applications. My research spans hardware-software co-design, operating system runtimes, and low-power sensor architectures. Recently, I've broadened my interests to include battery system architectures, audio and display pipelines, and sustainable technology. I hold an M.S. and Ph.D. from Arizona State University, where I was advised by Robert LiKamWa, and earned my undergraduate degree from the National Institute of Technology, Warangal.
